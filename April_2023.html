April 2023

Sunday, April 9th, 2023
Easter Sunday. Went for a visit to Craster village and Dustanburgh Castle. The drive was about 1 hrs 40. Parked at the main village car park and then went for a circular walk of 5 miles to Dunstanburgh Castle owned by English Heritage and round back to Craster village. We visited the castle and grounds and saw all the seagulls nesting in the cliffs, much to Harry's delight.
