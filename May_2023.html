Thursday, May 11th, 2023
It was Elena's final day at Droitwich today. Bernard had called to stay for several days on the way back from Penarth to help clean and pack boxes and to take a forward load of some items. Very wisely, everyone agreed to opt out of the self-rental option for a van and to use a small removal company instead. The removal team arrived to pack up the van, which they then kept overnight and set off at 5am to arrive in Penrith for 9.30am the following day. All went smoothly though and they had packed the van within an hour , which was amazing.
Friday, May 12th, 2023
It was the final part of the removal process today with the delivery of the large items to Penrith. Again, all went well. 1hour 30 for the removal team to unload. Lots going on as one of the internet companies was also installing a router and several of the other trades people were tidying a few items, so a busy,busy day.

Monday, May 15th, 2023
An update on the garden today as the weather is gradually improving and it is becoming a little warmer,after the recent cold spell, so the plants are coming on a bit now.We have created 2 new raised beds and there are more to come yet. Bernard has commenced and has been working on 'Project Gravel' which involves section by lifting and cleaning of the gravelled outdoor areas, laying weed-control membrane and then replacing the gravel. It also involved the purchase of a large quantity of new gravel for the front drive to improve the appearance at the front.
In terms of the productive garden/allotment, the first new raised bed contains lettuce, beetroot and radishes.The second raised bed contains rhubarb, peas, leeks, radishes and parsley. There are also 2 apple trees and a plum tree. In the greenhouse are tomatoes and peppers and the seedlings being propagated.
